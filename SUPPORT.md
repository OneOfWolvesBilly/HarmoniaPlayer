# Harmonia Player Support

Harmonia Player is a local music player for macOS.

## Contact

For support, bug reports, or feature requests, contact:

harmonia.audio.project@gmail.com

You can also report issues on GitHub:

https://github.com/OneOfWolvesBilly/HarmoniaPlayer/issues

## When reporting an issue

Please include:

- macOS version
- Harmonia Player version
- Audio file format
- A short description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible

## Privacy

Harmonia Player does not require an account, does not track users, and does not upload your music.

Your audio files stay on your Mac.
