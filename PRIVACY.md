# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 23, 2026_

Harmonia Player is a local music player for macOS. This Privacy Policy explains how Harmonia Player handles your information.

## Summary

Harmonia Player does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data with us or any third party. Any data the app saves — such as your playlists and preferences — stays on your Mac and is never sent off your device.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. Harmonia Player has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. The app does not include any analytics or crash-reporting services.

## File Access

Harmonia Player only accesses the audio files, playlists, and related files (such as artwork and lyrics) that you explicitly choose to open or import. This access happens entirely on your device. Your files are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with us or any third party.

## Network Access

Harmonia Player runs in the macOS sandbox with no network access. The app does not connect to the internet and does not send any data off your device.

## Third Parties

Harmonia Player does not sell, rent, trade, or share any data with third parties. Because the app collects no data, there is nothing to sell or share.

## Children's Privacy

Harmonia Player does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
